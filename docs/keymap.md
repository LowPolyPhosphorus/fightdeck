# Keymap

Standard MAME 4-Player keyboard layout,  RetroPie/MAME should recognize this out of the box. 

| Player | Up | Down | Left | Right | Btn1 | Btn2 | Btn3 |
|--------|----|------|------|-------|------|------|------|
| P1 | Up Arrow| Down Arrow | Left Arrow | Right Arrow | L-Ctrl | L-Alt| Space |
| P2 | R | F | D | G | A | S | Q |
| P3 | I | K | J | L | Rshift | Enter | P | 
| P4 | Num 8 | Num 2 | Num 4 | Num 6 | Num 0 | Num . | Num Enter |
